Double Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition, and then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop the dough, drop spoonfils of dough onto prepared backing sheet, leaving space between each
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still soft
  9. Cool on a wire rack before enjoying!

Tips!

  • Chill the dough for 30 minutes to prevent excessive spreading and enhance the flavor and texture.
  • Watch the baking time carefully, removing cookies when the edges are set but the center is slightly underbaked for a fudgy, chewy result.
