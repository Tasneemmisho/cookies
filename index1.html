Classic Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175'C.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla, beat in the eggs a time then add the vanilla
  3. Combine dry ingredients, in a saparate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda and salt. Gradually add to the wet ingredients, mixing until combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips
  5. Scoop the dough, drop spoonfils of dough onto prepared backing sheet, leaving space between each
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or intil the edges are golden brown
  7. Cool on a wire rack before enjoying!

Tips!

  • For chewy cookies, take them out slightly underbaked and let them cool on the tray.
  • Add nuts or swap out chocolate chips for variety!
